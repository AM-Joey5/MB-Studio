Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Most projects take 2-4 weeks from start to finish. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment options. Contact us to discuss a payment plan that works for your budget.

Can I request revisions?

Absolutely! Revision rounds are included in all our service packages. Check your specific plan for details on revision limits.

Do you work with remote clients?

Yes, we work with artists worldwide. We utilize modern technology to collaborate remotely while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all major formats: WAV, MP3, FLAC, and more. We'll work with you to ensure compatibility with your needs.

How do I get started?

Simply fill out the contact form above or call us directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project and goals.
