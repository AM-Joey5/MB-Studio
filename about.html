Our story

How we started

From a spare bedroom to three rooms downtown.

MB Studio began in 2014 in a converted garage with one microphone, a secondhand console and a founder who couldn't afford studio time anywhere else. Word travelled through Nashville's songwriter circuit before the paint had dried.

Today we run three rooms across a converted warehouse — but the reason people book us hasn't changed: we still treat every session like the record depends on it, because it does.

Mission

Give every artist studio-grade craft, regardless of budget or genre.

We built flexible booking and gear packages so a solo songwriter and a touring band get the same level of engineering attention.

Vision

Become the room where Nashville's next generation of records start.

We're investing in new formats — immersive mixing, podcast production, artist development — so MB Studio grows with the way music actually gets made now.

Milestones

Studio timeline

2014

A garage, one mic, one console

MB Studio opens as a single-room project studio, mostly recording local songwriters after hours.

2017

Room B opens

A second, larger live room is built to handle full-band tracking and drum recording.

2019

Mastering suite added

MB Studio brings mastering in-house, closing the loop from tracking to delivery-ready masters.

2022

Podcast & voice-over booth

A dedicated dialogue booth is added as more clients bring spoken-word and podcast work.

2024

Artist development program launches

MB Studio begins offering release strategy and development support alongside production.
