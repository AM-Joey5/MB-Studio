About The Studio

Where raw ideas become finished records.

MB Studio is an independent recording and production house built for artists who want their sound treated like a craft, not a template. From the first scratch vocal to the final master, every session is engineered around one goal: making the record sound like it belongs on the radio you grew up on.

Our mission is simple — protect the artist's vision, sharpen it with technical precision, and deliver a finished product that competes on any global platform. No shortcuts, no recycled presets, no rushed mixes. Just a disciplined, professional approach to every project that walks through our doors.

What We Do

Services built around your record

Recording

Vocal booths and live-room tracking with analog-warmed signal chains for full bands, solo artists, and features.

Mixing

Balanced, translatable mixes with depth and clarity — tuned to sit perfectly on club systems and earbuds alike.

Mastering

Loudness, tone, and cohesion calibrated to streaming standards so your release competes anywhere.

Beat Production

Custom instrumentals across genres — built from scratch or shaped around your topline and reference.

Podcast Production

Full-episode editing, cleanup, and sound design so your show sounds broadcast-ready every week.

Voice Recording

Voiceover, ADR, and narration capture with pristine isolation for ads, film, and audiobooks.

Artist Development

Long-term guidance on sound identity, catalog strategy, and release planning built around your career.
